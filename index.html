The basics of Apple products from an Advisor's persective

Apple is known for top notch support, but did you know that Apple Advisors have a simple procedure they follow? Apple Advisors use these general procedures to help you solve most of your issues you may experience with an iPhones, iPads, and iPods

What are the troubleshooting rules?

Apple Advisors use a 5 R's to a resolution that applies to most devices that support the iOS system.

  • Recharge
  • Restart
  • Reinstall
  • Restore
  • Repair

When it comes to repair, there are normally 4 options you have to replace it through the Apple corporation. Another option can be your carrier if there is a cellular package and insurance.
